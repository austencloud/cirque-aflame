1. FEES PAYABLE TO THE PERFORMER

  • Total fee for the performance is $1,200.00 USD.
  • Full payment of $1,200.00 USD is due on the day of performance.
  • Payment will be made by business check.
  • 'Completion' is defined as the end of the scheduled 3-hour performance or the moment the Performers cease performing due to unforeseen circumstances. Full payment remains due unless otherwise agreed in writing.

2. SERVICES

  • Event: Wicker Park Halloween Festival
  • Performance Details: 3-hour street performance featuring two performers:
    • Stilt-walking Jack Skellington character
    • Austen Cloud - Juggling pirate character
  • Performance Schedule:
    • 12:45 PM: Performers arrive for setup
    • 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Street performance
  • Costumes: Professional Halloween-themed costumes (Jack Skellington and pirate).
  • Equipment: All performance equipment will be provided by the Performers, including stilts and juggling props.

3. CANCELLATION AND REFUND

  • If Client cancels, Cirque Aflame will offer a backup date within one year, subject to availability (more likely with 2+ weeks notice).
  • If Producer cancels, payment may be applied toward rescheduling or waived entirely.
  • For severe weather, health emergencies, or force majeure events, both parties will work together to reschedule, modify, or cancel the performance as appropriate.

4. LATE ARRIVAL & CHANGE OF SCHEDULE

  • Full fee remains due even if performance is shortened at Client's request.
  • Performers will accommodate schedule delays under 30 minutes. Longer delays may result in modified performance duration, with full fee remaining due.
  • Changes to performance date or time must be communicated as soon as possible. Changes within 48 hours are subject to performer availability.

Client:
